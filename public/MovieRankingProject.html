The Best Movies According to Kenan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Wind Rises

A Japanese anime movie that tells the story of an aircraft engineer who designed a fighter plane for the imperial Japanese Airforce. That would be crashing into American ships at the coming end of WWII.

Star Wars

A plagarized Wizard of OZ like sci-fi/fantasy movie that is to overrated and over praised.

Train to Busan

A Korean Zombie Movie set on a train where characters have to survive a Zombie Apocalypse but to also advertise a train company.
